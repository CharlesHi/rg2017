Much of Robin's work in ceramics was done in classes where she was preparing students to make an ocarina. So these are demonstration pieces intended more to demonstrate the process of making than to produce a finished work.

Silly Notice: The things under this page are pictures of ceramic things. If you follow a link from this page you get to an enlarged image, and there you will be able to download a copy of the original image. But there aren't instructions on how to make one, and the images aren't useful for 3-d printing.

Pots: Containers designed to have lids

Most of these pots will have the lid in place. In some of them I set the lid ajar to allow you to see the join. In a couple of instances I actually removed the lid. And I believe that some of the lids are missing, perhaps broken.
