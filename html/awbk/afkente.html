Award Bookmark African Doll with Kente Cloth Example

This is a sample of a doll that can be created from a bookmark. Note that the doll was punched from one color paper and used with a frame from a different color paper. Also notice that the "kente cloth" was hand colored.

download this graphic

Note that the displayed size probably does not match the size of the original, and, of course, that the creation of the doll was blended with an appropriate legend. There is also given an appropriate song, but that isn't stressed.
