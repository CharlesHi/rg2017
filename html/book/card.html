Card Game

This booklet is a collection of simple projects that can be done with greeting cards. It is one of her earlier works, among those I have access to. There were many much earlier, but they seem to have been totally lost.

Note that the page numbers of the book do not match the project numbers. This is not a mistake. Some projects take up more than one page, and I didn't want to change numbering for the introduction. Most of the booklets will be numbered pages sequentially with the cover page counting as page 0. This booklet was printed on both sides of the paper, so the inside of the cover (which happens to be blank) got it's own page number.
